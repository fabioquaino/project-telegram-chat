
In this directory (Local), files for necessary connections (e.g., to databases) should be placed. These files must be named in the format: connection_name.properties (example: conexiones.properties, and inside, they should appear as follows:

[CONTEXT]
TELEGRAM_TOKEN=XXXXXXXXX
TELEGRAM_BOT_NAME=XXXXXXXXX
DB_HOST=XXXXXXXXX
DB_USER=XXXXXXXXX
DB_PASS=XXXXXXXXX
DB_NAME=XXXXXXXXX
TIME_ZONE=America/Argentina/Cordoba
